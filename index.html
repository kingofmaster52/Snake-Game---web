How to play :


1. Objective:


. Guide the snake to eat food and grow longer. Avoid hitting the walls, obstacles, or the snake's own body. .

2. Controls:


Use the arrow keys (or WASD) to move the snake:

Up Arrow (W): Move up

Down Arrow (S): Move down

Left Arrow (A): Move left

Right Arrow (D): Move right


3. Gameplay:


.
The snake moves continuously, and you can change its direction to collect the food.

Each time the snake eats food, it grows longer, and your score increases.

Be careful not to crash into the walls or the snake's body, as it will end the game.


4. Scoring:



Every piece of food adds points to your score.

Try to achieve the highest score possible! br

5. Winning and Losing:



There is no end to the game; the goal is to survive and score as much as possible.

The game ends when the snake collides with the wall or itself.br
